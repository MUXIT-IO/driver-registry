AudioSynth

v1.0.1 DLL utilities by muxit

Software audio synthesizer. Plays tones, musical notes, and short melodies on the server host's default audio output — useful for lab-side audible notifications, robot/CNC feedback tones, and experiment-complete beeps. Four waveforms (sine, square, triangle, sawtooth), volume control, and click-free envelopes.
